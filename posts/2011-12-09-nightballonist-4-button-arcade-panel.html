Working with my great pal Konstantino, we built an arcade style button mashing controller for Miguel Sternberg at SpookySquid Games. The button box was built for a really fantastic arcade game The Night Ballonists and was shown at the Hand Eye Society’s social #11. The innards of the button box involved hacking an Xbox controller by soldering connections between the xbox PCB and the arcade buttons.
